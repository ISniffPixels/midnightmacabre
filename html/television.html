Better Than the Comic Book Adaptation

The Sandman is one of comics' most acclaimed and beloved properties. Why a TV Show Instead of a Comic Book?

The Sandman's large cast, intricate plot, and literary allusions make it virtually impossible to adapt into a single comic book series. Weak Writing and a Terrible Adaptation

Unfortunately, Netflix's Castlevania also has a few weak spots. While the show's first season is pretty solid and has a lot of promise, the second season is a complete disaster. There are a few different reasons for this, but the biggest problem with the second season is that it fails to adapt the game's story into a compelling narrative. The game's story was incredibly intricate and interesting, with a ton of twists and turns. Unfortunately, the second season of Netflix's Castlevania just glosses over the majority of the game's story, and it barely touches on most of the twists that the game featured. The show squanders its potential by failing to adapt the game's story and instead opting for a much simpler narrative.

Mediocre animation quality and CGI choreography

Another problem with Netflix's Castlevania is its progressive downgrading of quality in regards to animation and CGI choreography. The show tries to save money by animating the characters in a way that looks like they are made of paper. Moreover, many of the show's fights feature poor CGI choreography, making them look very awkward and tacky. Unfortunately, this is something that could have been fixed with a little more time and money, so it's a real shame that they opted to cut corners here.

The Verdict

All things considered, Netflix's Castlevania is a mixed bag. The show has a few problems, and it has been rushed into production. However, it is still a fun show that seems to be very aware of what it wants to be, and it does its best within the constraints of its medium. If you are someone who has never played the Castlevania games, you should still be able to enjoy this show. This is also a great show for Castlevania fans who might be a bit more critical of it. Overall, this show is not a masterpiece, but it is an enjoyable watch nonetheless. If you are a fan of Castlevania, then you will probably enjoy this show, but you should not expect it to be an accurate adaptation of the game.

Rating: 4 / 5
